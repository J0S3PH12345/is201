The Correct Cosmere Ranking (according to me)


by Joseph


The Cosmere Universe by Brandon Sanderson is one of the greatest works of fantasy ever! So, it's time to rank the Cosmere novels that I've read! Spoiler free!!

This ranking doesn't include works included in the Arcanum Unbound collection (too many stories to list!), Rhythm of War, Yumi and the Nightmare Painter, and The Sunlit Man (I haven't read those yet)



For those who haven't read any of Brandon Sanderson's books, here's a little video of him explaining a little about his books and suggestions on where to start!




Now... onto the Ranking!!!



  1. Mistborn

      -So unique and intriguing
      -Got me hooked on the Cosmere
      -The classic Sanderson novel

  2. The Words of Radiance

      -Huge page turner
      -Action, Character development, magic... all are amazing

  3. Oathbringer

      -Amazing in general
      -It's crazy what happends in the final hundred pages! Who doesn't love the Sanderlanche?

  4. The Way of Kings

      -Amazing world building
      -It honestly could be higher, but the slowness of it holds it back a teeny tiny bit

  5. Warbreaker

      -It's probably a hot take putting Warbreaker this high, but I love it so much!
      -It's my favorite of the stand alone novels that Sanderson has written so far
      -Super cool magic system, so much intrigue in the plot, and I had a ton of fun trying to figure it all out

  6. The Lost Metal

      -Probably the most Cosmerey book of them all
      -So many twists and turns!
      -Fantastic resolution to the characters of Wax and Wayne that I grew to love, especially Wayne's resolution

  7. The Hero of Ages

      -Wow. What an epic conclusion to an epic story!
      -Sanderson ends this story in a way most authors don't, while setting up so much more with the Cosmere!

  8. The Alloy of Law

      -Classic western vibe, but with Mistborn magic?? Count me in!!
      -Wax and Wayne are the best duo of characters ever... change my mind. Sike, you can't.

  9. The Bands of Mourning

      -Fantasy Indiana Jones? YESSSSS!!!!
      -Awesome cliffhanger!
      -It's too slow at times, otherwise it's fantastic!

  10. The Well of Ascension

      -It's so cool to see how the heroes react to the events of the first book and build from there
      -Just like the first Mistborn, this book is amazing! Ugh, you guys, ranking Sanderson's books is so hard! They're really all number one

  11. Shadows of Self

      -Combining Dark Knight like suspense with amazing characters that I came to love in Alloy
      -Even though I love it so much, the tone just feels off and inconsistent

  12. Tress of the Emerald Sea

      -Such a wholesome story!
      -It feels simpler than most of the other works in the Cosmere, which is refreshing, but because of that, the ending is a bit predictable

  13. Elantris

      -The amazing plot, intriguing characters, and high stakes make this book stand out!
      -It can be an extremely slow read up until about halfway through, then it really picks up speed
      -With that being the case, the book is still amazing and 100% worth reading!
